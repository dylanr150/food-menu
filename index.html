Lumpia (Filipino Spring Rolls)

Ingredients

  • Ground pork or chicken
  • Carrots
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Green onions
  • Soy sauce
  • Salt & pepper
  • Lumpia wrappers (contains wheat/gluten)
  • Cooking oil (for frying)

Allergens to note:

  • ➡️ Gluten (wrapper, soy sauce)
